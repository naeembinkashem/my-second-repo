Author: Mohammad Mobarak Hossen Naeem

Once upon a time i and my frineds went to visit cox's bazar. we went there by train. we enjoyed our train journery.we played palying card during the train journey. at 6:00 am we reached at chattogram railway station then we had our breakfast and we booked bus ticket to go cox's bazar city. when we reached there first we booked a hotel. then after took some rest we went out to the sea beach. actually we were very excited to see the largest sea beach in the world. when i see it for the first time i was fasicnated to see for the fist time in my life. i enjoyed very well.
